Who We Are

IBDM International is a premier business development and marketing consultancy specializing in global market expansion across key industries including mining, garment manufacturing, construction, oil & gas, labor export, coffee, and agriculture.

Founded in 2010 by Tensae Moges Tegegn, we've grown from a regional consultancy to a global partner with offices in 3 continents and successful projects in over 25 countries.
